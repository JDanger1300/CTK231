A Bit About Me

I am a musician and creator from the greater Chicago area. I play guitar, bass, and sing. While the majority of my inerests and hobbies stem from music and sound, I have branched out to several other creative outlets. I make short films, be it acting, sound, or a number of other roles depending on the project. I dabble in 3D design and printing, visual art, primarily poster/wall art making, and general shenaniganizing. If I find it fun, I'll mess around with it, regardless of my skill level.
